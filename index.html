About Us

At Holidae, we are passionate about turning every person travel dream into reality. With over 8 years of experience, we specialize in curating personalized itineraries for leisure and group travelers. Our team of expert ensures every detail—from flights to accommodation—is handled with care, offering a 24/7 support to ensure a stress-free journey. We offer a bundled travel services combining flights, accommodation, a guided tour with transfer services into just one price. We also offer cruises and other family getaways. A trip to Dubai desert oasis and beach vacations at Maldives or a European luxury tour package. We turn trips into memories. Your travel partner for life Holidae.
